18 Days Annual Leave12 Days Sick Leave5 Public + 5 Festival Holidays Apply via KEKA
  • Annual Leave: 18 days per calendar year, with 4.5 days allocated per quarter.
  • Carryover: Up to 45 days of unused annual leave can be carried forward.
  • Casual & Sick Leave: 12 days per year, 3 days per quarter. Cannot be carried forward.
  • Public Holidays: 5 mandatory national holidays + 5 festival holidays announced annually.
  • Maternity Leave: 26 weeks (8 before + 18 after delivery). 12 weeks for 2+ children.
  • Paternity Leave: 5 days, within 3 months of birth or adoption.
  • Notice period: No leave permitted. Pre-approved leaves are automatically cancelled.
  • How to apply: Raise all requests through the KEKA portal.
12% Employee + 12% EmployerAction Required on Day 1
  • What is EPF? Both you and the company each contribute 12% of basic salary every month.
  • UAN: Your permanent PF identity number. Share existing UAN with HR immediately on joining.
  • Activate your UAN at the EPFO member portal using Aadhaar, PAN, or Member ID.
  • Link Aadhaar to UAN: Go to Manage → KYC in the EPFO portal — mandatory for all transactions.
  • Documents to submit: Aadhaar card, PAN card, and bank account details with IFSC code.
  • Nominate a family member on the EPFO portal (Manage → e-Nomination).

Zero ToleranceConfidential ReportingWhistleblower Protection
  • Integrity first: Every employee is expected to act honestly and ethically in all dealings.
  • Confidentiality: Client data, company financials, product roadmaps must be kept strictly confidential.
  • Raise concerns early: Report fraud, misuse of assets, compliance violations, or unethical behaviour.
  • How to report: Contact the Corporate Ombudsman at ethics@impactanalytics.co.
  • You are protected: Retaliation against anyone raising a concern in good faith is strictly prohibited.

Applies to EveryoneAll Work LocationsStrict Confidentiality
  • Zero tolerance: Committed to a workplace free from sexual harassment, discrimination, and abuse.
  • Scope: Covers offices, client sites, remote work, transport, off-sites, business trips, and social events.
  • How to file: Submit a written complaint to the Internal Committee within 3 months of the incident.
  • Confidentiality: All complaint details, identities, and findings are strictly confidential.
  • Interim relief: Transfer, reassignment, or up to 3 months of leave during inquiry.
